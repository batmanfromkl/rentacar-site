Insurance and Coverage
Rental Requirements
Cancellation Policy
Basic insurance:

All rentals include basic insurance, covering third-party liability.

Additional coverage:

Optional coverage, such as Collision Damage Waiver (CDW) and Personal Accident Insurance (PAI), can be purchased at an additional cost.

Excess Fees:

In the event of an accident, the renter may be liable for an excess fee unless additional coverage has been purchased.

Driver age:

Renters must be at least 21 years old to rent a standard vehicle. Luxury and premium vehicles require a minimum age of 25.

Driver’s license:

A valid driver’s license, held for a minimum of 1 year, is required to rent a vehicle.

Payment:

Full payment is required at the time of booking. We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express).

Free cancellation:

Cancellations made at least 24 hours before the pick-up time are eligible for a full refund.

Late cancellation:

If the cancellation is made less than 24 hours before the pick-up time, a one-day rental fee will be charged.

No-Show:

Failure to collect the car without prior notification will result in the full booking fee being charged.
